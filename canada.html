Frequently asked Questions

  • How long does it take for a Student Visa to process?

    Processing times vary depending on your country of residence and the volume of applications. Generally, SDS applications are processed within 20 calendar days, while non-SDS applications can take 8 to 12 weeks. We recommend applying at least 4 to 6 months before your intake begins to avoid any delays.

  • What is the fee structure for visa submission?

    The standard Canadian Study Permit fee is $150 CAD. Additionally, most applicants are required to pay a biometrics fee of $85 CAD. Please note that these are government fees and do not include university tuition deposits or medical exam costs.

  • What are the important things to know as a Student Visa applicant?

    The most critical factors are a valid Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) and proof of financial support (usually via a GIC). You must also demonstrate a clear "intent to return" to your home country after your studies, though Canada offers many legal routes to stay and work after you graduate.
